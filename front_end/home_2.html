What to look for

Deepfakes often leave subtle traces: inconsistent lighting, blur around edges, mismatched facial landmarks, and unnatural eye blinking. BeFR analyzes visual patterns and returns a confidence score.

Prototype uses a CNN backbone fine-tuned on balanced real/synthetic datasets (research/development). For production, combine audio verification and multiple ensemble detectors.
